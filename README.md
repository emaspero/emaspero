Junior Software Engineer

I am a bilingual Junior Software Developer with 3 years experience in managing sales and international clients in the UK’s New Homes industry and a bachelor degree in Languages. My path led me to study Languages in University and, to a certain extent, languages and programming languages have a lot in common, starting from the fact that they are not subjects that you learn and store away, but they are skills that you can actually put into practice. The problem-solving, analytical world of programming, with its endless learning possibilities, perfectly chimes with my attitude and my strong desire to always improve and widen my knowledge. A thorough research directed me to enroll in General Assembly’s Software Engineering Immersive course to help develop my self-taught skills and acquire a wider full-stack knowledge.
